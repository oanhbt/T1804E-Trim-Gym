Nordic Hamstring Curl

Effective post-thigh muscle training will help you increase your stretching ability to perform other exercises for the lower and lower body. better. If you want to focus on improving your thigh muscles, the Nordic Hamstring Curl is the perfect choice.

So please find someone to help you when doing this article.
  • After preparation and ready. At this time your body is straight perpendicular to the floor, legs parallel to the floor.
  • Slowly lower to the front and down parallel to the floor or lowest possible at the first.
  • Then strain the thigh laterally and pull the person back to its original position.

Standing Dumbbell Lateral Raise

  • An exercise for our middle shoulders. Please prepare two dumbbells before.
  • Stand straight, with two hands holding the two sides of the hips. The elbows bent slightly and fixed this curvature throughout the exercise.
  • Stretch the upper body, then exhale and lift the weights up high until the hands parallel the floor.
  • Slowly lower and inhale.
  • You can also replace the cable driver.

Reverse Pec Deck Fly

  • Sitting face in the machine, put two hands on the horizontal position.
  • Gradually expand the arms horizontally as far as possible.
  • Note that the arm still has a certain curvature.

Dumbbell Fly

  • A quick muscle boost for your inner chest. You can do this exercise on the machine in the article by turning the face out (if you are not familiar, should practice on the machine first), but the episode will feel more appreciated.
  • Lie on the floor, holding two dumbbells. Notice the arm is slightly curved and not stretched completely.
  • Hold 2 dumbbells in front of your chest.
  • Exhale, slowly extend your arms to the side, until you feel the chest muscles stretch.
  • Pull both hands into place and squeeze tight chest muscle and inhale.
