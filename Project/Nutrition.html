Nutrition in the body is always our theme, and more specifically for the new GYM training, nutrition knowledge in particular and all the bodybuilding knowledge in general is very limited. Exercise is the first necessary condition for muscle to develop and bring about a change in appearance, but nutrition is enough to determine whether you succeed or fail. People want to live and work should eat, but eat how to eat is not wise people do not know. Fat This is both a friend and a foe for us, you absolutely should not eat saturated fat or animal fat (except marine fat is a good fat is good for the heart). Keep in mind that fat in your daily diet, a small amount of fat also gives you a great deal of calories that affect your weight management.
